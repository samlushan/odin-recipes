Broccoli Beef

Ingredients

  • 1 lb beef, sliced thinly
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup water

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix soy sauce, oyster sauce, garlic, ginger, cornstarch, and water.
  2. Add beef to the marinade and let it sit for 30 minutes.
  3. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  4. Add marinated beef and cook until browned.
  5. Add broccoli and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes.
  6. Serve hot with rice or noodles.
