Orange Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken breast, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/2 cup orange juice
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix orange juice, soy sauce, sugar, garlic, ginger, and cornstarch.
  2. Add chicken pieces to the marinade and let it sit for 30 minutes.
  3. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  4. Add marinated chicken and cook until golden brown.
  5. Add remaining marinade to the pan and cook until thickened.
  6. Serve hot with rice or noodles.
